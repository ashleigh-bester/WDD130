Overview

Purpose

An informational website explaining the functions of the ThinkNature App. It is a unique platform that focuses on nature and allows families and individuals to participate in photography and videography competitions by completing a series of challenges, e.g. A scavenger hunt in the famous Kruger National Park. They do this via the ThinkNature app. The website includes tips, tricks, highlights and other information on how to use the app effectively, as well as child pages for each geographical area.

Audience

Families and Individuals of ALL AGES

Content

Home page

Welcome to ThinkNature! We would like to invite you on an adventure to create your own Ecosystem and ultimately strengthen your 'Beauty Fitness'. Once you have downloaded the ThinkNature App you can begin participating in various challenges, as a family, group, or as an individual. We are stoked to see what you come up with! Please visit 'Your Ecosystem' for tips and tricks on getting started.

Images for the Home page

Your Ecosystem

Your Ecosystem is where you will find the content you create. It contains your profile information such as posts, followers, votes, comments, etc. It is your space to share your natural environment with people from around the world. This includes wildlife, scenery, nature conservation, outdoor activities, etc. It does not matter where you live. We would love to see the beauty you find in your back garden too! Think 'Nature' and show us how you become 'Beauty Fit'.

Images for the Page 2

Kruger National Park

The birth place of ThinkNature is the famous Kruger National Park. It is one of the largest conservation areas in South Africa and attracts more than one million visitors each year. This area is rich with wildife, including the Big Five! Car rides can get long for little ones, sometimes even for the adults. We tend to look for the obvious attractions, such as the Big Five, but they aren't always reliable. ThinkNature provides activities that changes your perspective on what the main attraction is. These activities are designed to keep everyone engaged, involved and challenge your 'Beauty Fitness'. [Facts about Kruger and ThinkNature challenges listed below p]
