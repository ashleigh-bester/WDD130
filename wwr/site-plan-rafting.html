Ashleigh Bester

Overview

Purpose

To have a site that people can explore to find information on whitewater rafting as well as sign up to participate in this exciting sport, ultimately becoming an active community.

Audience

Adventurous people, age 14 and up, whose bodies can handle the rigorous exercise and look forward to spending the day with us. Club members will have the opportunity to join weekly expiditions. The site can be accessed via any mobile device.
